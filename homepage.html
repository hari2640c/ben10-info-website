Ben Tennyson

Benjamin Kirby “Ben” Tennyson is a 10 year old boy whose world changes when he discovers the Omnitrix, a powerful alien device that lets him transform into a variety of unique heroes.Though he starts off impulsive and playful, his adventures with Gwen and Grandpa Max help him grow into a braver and more responsible hero.

Omnitrix

The Omnitrix, a device that looks like a watch that is worn on Ben's wrist, is the main focus of the series. The gadget gives the user the ability to change their DNA at will and change into dozens of different alien species, each with unique abilities.The Omnitrix was a device created by Azmuth of the Galvan, in an effort to promote peace among species in the universe. The original found its way onto the wrist of then 10-year-old Ben Tennyson, who would go then use the device to save the world, universe, and multiverse, many times over.
